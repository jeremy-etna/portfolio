Fractal Rift

A real-time strategy game developed in Unity 6 (C#)

Gameplay Video

Gameplay

Fractal Rift is a real-time strategy game developed in Unity 6, where players control units with complementary abilities to explore a world fractured by dimensional rifts. Designed as a sandbox for systemic game design, Fractal Rift focuses on tactics, team management, and adapting to dynamic environments.

I created this project as a showcase of my skills in gameplay programming, software architecture (using C# with a modular and maintainable approach), and player-oriented UI/UX design. It allowed me to fully grasp the challenges of a complete production pipeline—from concept to deployment—while emphasizing code clarity, testing, and documentation.

This game is also a reflection of my ability to lead a technical project from start to finish, make informed design decisions, and adapt to production constraints. It demonstrates my passion for innovation and my commitment to crafting strategic and engaging experiences.
